Island of Tears

The Island of Tears is a memorial dedicated to Belarusian soldiers who fell during the war in Afghanistan.

This solemn monument is located on an artificial island in the Svislach River in central Minsk.

At the center of the memorial stands a chapel with sculptures of grieving mothers and widows.

The atmosphere here is profoundly moving, inviting visitors to reflect on the cost of war.

Many locals and tourists visit this site to pay respects and appreciate its artistic significance.

Crying Guardian Angel

The Crying Guardian Angel is a poignant sculpture located near the Island of Tears memorial.

This bronze statue depicts an angel with bowed head, symbolizing grief for fallen soldiers.

The sculpture captures a moment of profound sorrow and serves as a guardian for lost souls.

Its detailed craftsmanship and emotional expression make it a powerful artistic statement.

Visitors often leave flowers and tokens of remembrance at the base of this touching monument.

"City Scales" Sculpture

The "City Scales" sculpture is an interesting modern artwork located in Minsk's city center.

This unique installation features a large balance scale that represents justice and equilibrium.

Created by contemporary artists, it symbolizes the balance between different aspects of urban life.

The sculpture has become a popular photo spot for both tourists and local residents.

Its interactive nature allows visitors to engage physically with this thought-provoking artwork.
